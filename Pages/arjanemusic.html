Arjane Music

Portfolio Item: Branding for Musician Arjane

As part of this project, I had the exciting opportunity to create the branding for musician Arjane. Through in-depth research and collaboration, I developed a cohesive visual identity that authentically represented the musician's unique style and resonated with their target audience.

Brand Research - I conducted a thorough analysis of Arjane's personality, target audience, and market positioning to develop a brand strategy that aligned with their artistic vision and goals.
Logo and Visual Elements - I designed a distinctive logo and a set of visual elements, including color palettes, typography guidelines, and supporting graphics, that captured the essence of Arjane's identity.
Brand Guidelines - I meticulously documented the brand guidelines, providing clear instructions on logo usage , color combinations, typography hierarchy, and graphic treatments. This ensured consistency across various platforms and materials.
Web Development - I conceptualized and presented album artwork ideas that reflected the brand's visual identity , creating a visually captivating and cohesive album release experience.

I was able to build a I created for Arjane served for a more professional, cohesive and personal virtual identity.
